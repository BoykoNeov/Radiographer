> Fluence-to-dose conversion coefficients close the §6 photon dose chain: the engine
> forms a per-line fluence `Φ_i = y_i · A / (4π d²)`, then **H\*(10) = Σ Φ_i ·
> h\*(10)/Φ(E_i)** (operational, the survey-meter reading, §6.4 **default/primary**) or
> **E = Σ Φ_i · e/Φ(E_i, geometry)** (effective dose, per body orientation). This is the
> last *scoring-side* dataset the M3 gamma engine needs (the material side — attenuation
> + buildup — already landed).

## Scope (LOCKED for this dataset)

- **Photons only.** Both quantities are the photon fluence-to-dose coefficients.
  - **Neutron** H\*(10)/effective dose → the **separate** "neutron fluence-to-dose"
    §7 row, landing with the neutron source terms (the schema/loader here is shaped so a
    neutron file slots in via the `particle` field without a rewrite).
  - **Electron/positron** effective dose → **not shipped**: external beta is a *skin-dose*
    problem (§6.2, M4), handled by a point-kernel, not by whole-body E/Φ coefficients.
- **Two quantities, two vintages (both §6.4-LOCKED):**
  - **H\*(10)** ambient dose equivalent — **ICRP-74 (1996) / ICRU-57** vintage. *Single*
    column (operational quantity on the ICRU **sphere**; **no geometry**). Grid 0.01–10 MeV.
  - **Effective dose E** — **ICRP-116 (2010)** vintage (ICRP-103 tissue weights, voxel
    phantoms). **Per geometry** AP/PA/LLAT/RLAT/ROT/ISO. Grid 0.01 MeV–10 GeV.
- **Per-quantity grid — do NOT force a shared grid.** ICRP-74 photons stop at 10 MeV;
  ICRP-116 runs to 10 GeV. M3 treats an H\*(10) request **above 10 MeV** as off-grid
  (mirror the buildup 15 MeV contract), never extrapolated.

### Vintage pin (the trap the advisor flagged)
A 2026 search for "H\*(10) conversion coefficients" surfaces the **ICRU-95 / 2020**
operational-quantity revision, whose photon values **differ** from ICRU-57/ICRP-74. §6.4
locks the **ICRP-74/ICRU-57** vintage (the survey-meter-calibration standard). The shipped
H\*(10)/Ka has the ICRU-57 **sphere** signature — peak ≈ 1.77 near 60–80 keV — distinct
from both the new ICRU-95 quantity and the ICRU **slab** Hp(10,0°)/Ka (which peaks ≈ 1.90).

## Validation design (advisor-reviewed)

The effective table re-uses the **clean** model (verbatim → independent re-parse → goldens).
The H\*(10) table uses the **degraded** model and leans on an *independent-quantity*
cross-check, because no clean machine-readable H\*(10)/Φ exists.

**The H\*(10) cross-check (the crux).** OpenMC's *independently sourced* ICRP-74 **Table A.1
air-kerma-per-fluence** Ka/Φ (from `generate_photon_effective_dose.py`, the same module that
derives ICRP-74 effective dose) lies on the **same 25-energy grid** as the H\*(10)/Φ table.
So `H*(10)/Ka = (H*(10)/Φ) / (Ka/Φ)` is computable with **no interpolation**, and must
reproduce the canonical ICRU-57 **sphere** response. Confirmed against an **independent**
third source (IAEA training doc, slab Hp(10,0°)/Ka), which **coincides** with the sphere at
the energies where geometry doesn't matter and **diverges** where it should:

| E (MeV) | derived H\*(10)/Ka (PR ÷ OpenMC Ka/Φ) | IAEA Hp(10,0°)/Ka (slab) | regime |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.015 | 0.266 | 0.264 | low-E coincidence |
| 0.030 | 1.123 | 1.112 | low-E coincidence |
| 0.040 | 1.492 | 1.490 | low-E coincidence |
| 0.060 | 1.765 | 1.892 | **backscatter divergence** (sphere < slab) |
| 1.0 | 1.163 | 1.167 | MeV re-convergence |
| 3.0 | 1.114 | 1.117 | MeV re-convergence |

Three-way agreement (PR, OpenMC, IAEA — mutually independent) at **both ends and across the
decay-gamma working range 0.1–3 MeV** validates H\*(10)/Φ there regardless of how the PR was
produced. The mid-energy (50–200 keV) interior is checked **structurally** (peak 1.7–1.8 @
60–80 keV; monotone rise above the 60 keV minimum) since slab ≠ sphere there.

**Pillars (TDD — written first, confirmed failing, built to green):**
1. **Structural** — schema_version/quantity/geometry/particle/units, ascending E, positive
   finite coeffs, array alignment; H\*(10) has `geometry: null`, effective carries one of the 6.
2. **Transform integrity** — canonical == vendored, re-parsed from the raw `.txt` by an
   *independent* tokenizer (not the build's). Catches drop/dup/mis-row/value-mangle.
3. **Coverage** — all 6 effective geometries + H\*(10) present; both directions (no strays).
4. **Physics goldens (citable, not from memory):**
   - Effective: vendored AP coeffs vs a **separate group's** ICRP-116 piecewise-poly fit
     (Veinot et al., PMC6074822, ≤3 %) across 0.03–6 MeV (match ≤1.2 %), plus a
     geometry-ordering relation (AP > PA > ISO; ROT > ISO > RLAT at 1 MeV) that catches a
     column swap. OpenMC's own absolute values are **not** re-asserted as goldens — the
     transform-integrity pillar already proves canonical == OpenMC wholesale.
   - H\*(10): derived H\*(10)/Ka within ~1.5 % of **IAEA** at 0.015/0.030/0.040 (low-E
     coincidence) **and** 1.0/3.0 MeV (MeV re-convergence); structural mid-E peak/monotone.

## Caveats / honesty (surface in M3/UI)

- **H\*(10) > 3 MeV is higher-uncertainty.** The derived ratio drifts ~4 % below the slab by
  10 MeV (1.067 vs 1.111) — real sphere geometry at deep buildup, or the PR's high-E
  H\*(10)/Φ rounding progressively low. **Low-stakes:** decay gammas essentially never exceed
  ~2.6 MeV (Tl-208 2.614 MeV), inside the 3-way-validated range. Documented, not silently
  shipped as exact.
- **Trust boundary (honesty).** Effective: canonical == OpenMC (re-parse proven); OpenMC ==
  ICRP-116-incl-corrigendum (anchored at N energies, bulk trusted). H\*(10): transcribed from
  unmerged PR #3256, independently anchored (IAEA + OpenMC Ka/Φ) at low- and MeV-energies;
  the 50–200 keV interior and >3 MeV tail rest on the transcription. Same shape as the
  buildup/emissions golden-subset boundary.

## Carry-forward for M3 (gamma dose)
- **New scoring-side energy floor: 10 keV (0.01 MeV)** for *both* conversion quantities —
  *higher* than the 1 keV attenuation floor. Photon lines below 10 keV have no conversion
  coefficient: skip **explicitly and logged** (extend the existing sub-keV contract), never
  silent.
- **H\*(10) above 10 MeV is off-grid** (grid ends at 10 MeV); effective above 10 GeV.
  Handle explicitly + logged, never extrapolate (mirror the buildup 15 MeV contract).
- **Interpolation in log-E** (these coeffs are smooth, no edges) is the M3 engine's job;
  the loader ships none (mirrors attenuation/buildup).
- **Effective dose forces a geometry choice** (§6.4): expose the 6-way dropdown; default
  ISO or AP (§13 OPEN #3, a UI decision for M6 — this dataset ships all six).
